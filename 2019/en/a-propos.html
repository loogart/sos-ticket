Our mission

Focused on quick access to the justice system.

Our mission is to provide Quebec drivers with simple, fast and fair access to the justice system.

Furthermore, by exercising motorists’ rights, our mission implies a preventive duty that passes through the education and awareness of motorists regarding the Highway Safety Code.

The law can sometimes seem complicated. Its interpretation and application present several grey areas that citizens do not always have the time or ability to understand all the subtleties.

While the right to a full representation and defense is part of the foundation of our society, access to justice is a long and complex process which can quickly discourage the most motivated driver. That is why most drivers don’t go through the trouble of exercising their rights to benefit from the system put in place to protect them.

Believing that each case is unique, that it is important to examine all the circumstances related to an offense and that abandoning your defence by mutism is an injustice, SOS Ticket makes sure to analyze in detail each case presented.

Our expertise

We defend you.

Whether it is using a cell phone while driving, driving while impaired, passing a red light or high-speed driving, we do not only intend to defend your rights, but first and foremost to inform the public of potential dangers and consequences related to an offense.

By using the services of our law firm, you benefit from a privileged access to a team of lawyers and experienced experts who master the Highway Safety Code and who have a proven track record in contesting traffic tickets.
