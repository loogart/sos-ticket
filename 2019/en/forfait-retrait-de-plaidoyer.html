Motion to withdraw guilty plea

Legal assistance for a motion to withdraw a guilty plea

When a ticket is paid, the citizen is deemed to have transmitted a guilty plea and by that very fact a judgment making him guilty will be rendered. In some very specific cases, a citizen may have pleaded guilty in error or someone else may have done so on their behalf. In this situation, the only possible solution is an appeal to the Superior Court of Québec. Despite the strict requirements of this procedure, our lawyers will be able to guide you to allow you to have the right to a full and complete defense.

The services included in this package:

  • File opening
  • Legal analysis
  • Drafting of documents
  • Consultation at our offices
  • Deposit of documents
  • Notifying/Serving of documents
  • Representation at the Courthouse
